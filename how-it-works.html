How It Works

We offer many services for you in order to make this experience a stress free one. All you need to be aware of is that you will be having a medical procedure and we are here to organize, aid and guide you through the process so that you don’t have to organize your trip alone.

What We Organize

  • Flights, hotel accommodation booking
  • Vaccination information
  • Meet & Greet service - pick up and drop off at your destination airport, hotel and hospital
  • Accommodation for family members or assistants
  • Nurse or personal assistant if required to assist with your stay
  • Provide you with a Welcome Pack containing details of your travel, accommodation, procedure, appointment details, doctor's resume, pictures of the hospital, and general information
  • Organise a telephone conversation and further correspondence with the doctor who will be treating you and contact nurse for your entire procedure
  • Provide you and your family with contact numbers and support line for updates and feedback of your procedure
  • Interpreters if required (all our doctors and nurses speak English as standard and various languages)
