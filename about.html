About Dr. Arturo Corchuelo

Dr. Arturo Corchuelo is a physician with more than 35 years of experience in Orthopedics and Traumatology. He completed his studies at Universidad Javeriana and his specialization in Switzerland. He is a pioneer in Colombia implementing modern procedures with compassionate, patient-centered care.
